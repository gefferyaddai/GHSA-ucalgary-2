Why It Matters

Why support
GHSA?

Community contributions directly support student cultural programming and leadership development at the University of Calgary.

Our flagship events attract 300–500+ attendees including students, families, and community leaders — and your support makes them possible.

  • 24K+ views across 3 Instagram videos & stories
  • 1.2K+ interactions across posts and stories
  • 2,556+ profile visits
  • 4,969+ views on TikTok
  • 229+ shares across 3 videos
🇬🇭

Strengthen Cultural Identity

Promote Ghanaian heritage, create spaces where students feel connected to their culture, and celebrate Ghana through events and performances.

🚀

Support Student Growth

Help students develop leadership skills, access networking opportunities with professionals, and build community among Ghanaian and African students.

🌍

Build a Stronger Community

Connect students with the Ghanaian diaspora, encourage mentorship and intergenerational support, and strengthen cultural representation on campus.

🎉

Enable Meaningful Events

Fund Cultural Night, Ghana Independence Celebrations, Networking & Career Events, Food Festivals, and community-building social events.
