About Us

A home away
from home

The Ghana Students Association at the University of Calgary was created to build a home away from home for Ghanaian students and anyone interested in Ghanaian culture. GHSA was founded to strengthen community, celebrate culture, and create a space where students can connect, support one another, and feel represented on campus.

Through events, networking, mentorship, and shared experiences, GHSA continues to grow as a student-led community committed to belonging, leadership, and cultural pride.

Our Mission

Culture, community,
and opportunity

Our mission is to create a welcoming and empowering space for students through culture, community, and opportunity. GHSA aims to support students socially, academically, and professionally while promoting Ghanaian identity and excellence throughout campus life.

As a club, we strive to host meaningful events, encourage student involvement, build long-lasting connections, and provide a platform where members can grow in confidence, leadership, and unity.
